The 19th Century was a period when Europe and the world experienced rapid and profound changes in all areas. Art, and especially painting, in the 19th Century was no different. The changes over the course of 100 years were dramatic, transitioning from historic ‘Old Masters’ style works, to the dawn of Modernity.
